Privacy Policy - Discord Bots

1. What data do we collect?

Our bots may collect the following information:

  • Message content sent in the form of commands to the bot.
  • User statuses on the server to adjust the bot's functions.
  • Information about users in voice channels to manage music playback properly.

2. How do we use the collected data?

The collected data is used solely to ensure the bot functions correctly and to provide features related to music playback. This includes:

  • Executing music commands such as playing, pausing, or skipping tracks.
  • Managing the presence of users in voice channels to automatically start and stop music playback.

3. Do we store data outside of Discord?

No, Our bots does not store any data outside of Discord. All processed information is temporary and is used only to handle real-time commands.

4. Do we share your data with others?

No, Our bots does not share any data with third parties or external services.

6. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to make changes to this Privacy Policy. Any changes will be announced in advance through the appropriate communication channels.
